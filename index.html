Cee-lo

The rules:

  • First to 10 wins the game.
  • If a pair is rolled the odd die is the point value. Possible point values range from 1 to 6.
  • Trips of any number beat any pair.
  • If player rolls a 4 5 6 they automatically win the round.
  • If a player rolls a 1 2 3 they automatically loose the round and must give the opposing player a point form their score.
